Our Heritage

Our Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1875 in the heart of Switzerland, our company has been crafting exceptional timepieces for over 145 years. Our commitment to excellence has been passed down through five generations of master watchmakers.

Each timepiece tells a story of innovation, precision, and unparalleled craftsmanship that has defined luxury watchmaking for nearly one and a half centuries.

The Art of Craftmanship

Precision Engineering

Each movement consists of over 300 hand-finished components, meticulously assembled by our master craftsmen. Every gear, spring, and bridge is carefully selected and finished to perfection.

Master Assembly

Every timepiece takes over 500 hours to complete, with each component being carefully inspected and assembled by hand to ensure perfect functionality and reliability.

Quality Control

Rigorous testing ensures perfection in every detail. Each watch undergoes extensive quality control processes before being certified for release.
